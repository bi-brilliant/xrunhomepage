SUMMARY

Advertiser Problem

In order to make more profit, the agency intentionally manipulates the number of queries and provides statistical data on the manipulation rather than the actual user clicks. Therefore they are reluctant to advertise online as users are on the trend of not looking at ads.

User Problem

Illegal pornographic ads, fraud ads (if they’re not real) and spam ads are causing people to lose faith. As a result, the ecosystem in the advertising market is crumbling as users are not interesting, are closing ads without seeing them, or trying not to see them through programs that are completely invisible.

Advertiser Solutions

Due to an recording on the blockchain of advertising activities, the advertising agency cannot manipulate and data can be received from the advertising agency. As a result, it is possible to pay a reasonable adverting fee as much as the user’s performance.

User Solutions

Through monitoring that is possible to prevent illegal advertising, frau, etc. from entering the XRUN advertising platform, AR technology was grafted together to enable users to be as interested as a game in watching advertisements as well as to providing good quality advertisements to users.
